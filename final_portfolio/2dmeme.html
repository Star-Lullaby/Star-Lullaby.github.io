2D Meme

2026

This project investigates meme culture through digital composition, humor, and remixing. The work combines online visual language with graphic experimentation to create an intentionally exaggerated internet aesthetic, via Adobe Photoshop. As you can see, this image takes the iconic "Weezer" album cover, and replaces it's members with the adorable characters from the "Chiikawa" series. I was inspired to make this piece purely for the silliness of the idea, as I am a big fan of "Chiikawa", and the way the characters are posed was an opportunity too perfect to pass up on.
