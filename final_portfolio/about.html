About the Artist

I am an artist focused on traditional art, digital experimentation, internet aesthetics, multimedia storytelling, and sculpture.

My work explores remix culture, humor, visual distortion, and personal expression through both digital and physical media. Projects range from hypertext experiences and glitch art to sculptural assemblages made from recycled materials.

This portfolio was created as a curated space to document artistic growth and present projects created for coursework, experimentation, and personal interest.
