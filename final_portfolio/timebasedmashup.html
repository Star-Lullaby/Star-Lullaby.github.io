Time-Based Mashup

2026

This video mashup combines edited footage, audio layering, and montage techniques to create a fast-paced multimedia experience. The project focuses on rhythm, timing, and transformation through editing. The title of my work is "Unrestricted Internet Access". Made in Adobe Premiere, the video is a collage of various internet horrors played in rhythm with the song "Hidden in the Sand", by Tally Hall, that explores what it was like growing up as a kid who had unrestricted access to the internet. From famous "Creepypastas" to infamous YouTube videos, my video is sure to relate to those who met a similar fate.
