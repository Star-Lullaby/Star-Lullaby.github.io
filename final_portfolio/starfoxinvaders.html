Interactive Javascript-based video game project.

Fly in to space with Star Fox Invaders

This project explores the unique coding experience of p5.js to make a fun, interactive game. The game is Space Invaders, but with certain elements from the game series "Star Fox". I came up with idea as being a fun way of modernizing an iconic game from the past, for modern viewers to enjoy!
