 Synopsis 


ENA, is a Peruvian avant-garde animated comedy series created by digital media artist Joel G.

Published on YouTube and Newgrounds, the series combines 2D and 3D animation, drawing inspiration

from many pieces of surrealist media and worldwise culture, particularly from Peru and Japan. 

It is set in a bizarre, allsmost post-apocalyptic world populated 

by strange characters and creatures with a surreal and comedic tone.
