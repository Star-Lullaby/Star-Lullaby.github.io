GAME RULES

The goal of this adventure is to build a powerful bond with your Pokémon by winning battles and helping them evolve to their greatest potential.

How to Play:

• Choose your starter Pokémon to begin your journey.

• You and your Pokémon will face two battle stages against different opponents.

• During each round, select the opposing Pokémon that has a type disadvantage against your starter Pokémon.

• Winning a battle rewards your Pokémon with EXP points and allows it to evolve into its next form.

• Choosing the wrong opponent will result in defeat, and you must restart your journey from the beginning.

• Successfully complete all three stages and fully evolve your Pokémon to become a true Pokémon Master!
