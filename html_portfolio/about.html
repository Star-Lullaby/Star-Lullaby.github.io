About Me

In my work, I am drawn to both designing original characters and interpreting existing ones. Appearance plays an important role in how a character is understood; costume, silhouette, and small details can suggest backstory and mood. By utilizing drawing and costume making, I am able to experiment with stylization, exaggeration, and tone. These interests reflect my focus on creativity, individuality, and the enjoyment of translating imagination into something visible for others to see. My process includes utilizing pencil or pen on paper when illustrating, or foam, fabric, and sewing materials when constructing wearable pieces. Through drawing and costume making, I explore how I can use different materials and methods to bring characters to life in distinct but equally meaningful ways.
